Round Selection

Overview

The Round Selection tool rounds the corners of the active selection area.

This tool has no effect on circular selections.

Round Selection Options

Round Selection Settings

Roundness

This setting controls the amount of rounding that will be performed on the selection. Higher values will render the selection with more roundedness.

See also

This filter is most commonly used in conjunction with the Rectangular Selection tool.

Tileable Blur

Overview

The Tileable Blur filter can be utilized to blur already tileable images so that the tileable properties of the layer are not lost after blurring.

Tileable Blur Settings

The Radius slider sets the radius of the blur in the same fashion as the Gaussian Blur IIR , or the Gaussian Blur RLE filters. The Blur Type dropdown sets the blur to either IIR, or RLE.

GIH

Overview

The GIMP uses the filetype GIH for animated brushes.

Details

The GIH fileformat, or GIMPImageHose, is a greyscale format and stores multiple layers. At the time of rendering the brush onto a drawable, The GIMP renders each of these layers in turn to achieve an animated brushing.

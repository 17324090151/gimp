Center Guide

Overview

The Center Guide filter places a horizontal and a vertical guide on your image.

Center Guide Options

Center Guide Settings

Physical

This checkbox will place the guides in the exact pixel center of the image.

Optical

This checkbox will place the guides in the optical center of the image. The optical center differs in that it represents more what the human eye and brain visualize as the center of the image.

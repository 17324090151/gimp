Snap to Guides

This option toggles snap to guides. Snapping to guides has the effect of pulling the pointer or cursor to a guide if the guide is within a certain threshold of the pointer. The threshold is based on screen distance, not image distance. This means that at higher zoom values, snapping may be harder to achieve. This feature allows mathematical control when drawing shapes that may rely on precision alignment.

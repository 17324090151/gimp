Invert

This command will render the colors of the active layer or current selection inversely to their previous values. Normal colors will be rendered to the opposite color as described on a color wheel, whereas black, gray, and white will be inverted according to saturation.

There is a color wheel included with The GIMP and it can be found in the GTK Color Selection dialog .
